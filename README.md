## Key Business Insights
1. Customers with monthly contracts experienced the highest churn rate.
2. Higher monthly charges were associated with increased customer churn.
3. UPI payment users showed comparatively higher churn behavior.
4. Long-tenure customers demonstrated stronger retention patterns.
5. Male customers showed slightly higher churn counts compared to female customers.

## Business Recommendations
1. Encourage long-term subscription plans with discounts.
2. Improve retention campaigns for high-charge customers.
3. Offer loyalty benefits for long-tenure customers.
4. Improve payment flexibility and customer support.
5. Target high-risk customer segments with personalized offers.
